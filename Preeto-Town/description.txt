Preeto Town Hub
===============
A personal community portal for the fictional town of Preeto.

Login — three types: Mallow, Person, Community.
  Persons: Henry and Margaret (hardcoded codes); other owners register via Person login.
Dashboard — scrolling news from news.txt with date-gating formulas.
Shops — CSV-driven menu, XML-driven worker discounts, cart system.
Mallow Controls — lend mallows, set downtime limits.

Data files: shops.csv, workers.xml, news.txt, owners.json
Registry: Henry owns S/Preeto/Kitticorn/Tally/Tie-dye Tally/Chea/Hawk/Townes
          Margaret owns Perl/Green/Jameel/Benny/Froggy/Holly/Olivette/R
S = Preeto = mayor. Tally = Tie-dye Tally = Kitticorn.

Mallow family (the Keyring family) — sizes from sizes.txt:
  Adult  — S (dad), R (mum)
  Teen   — Townes, Kitticorn
  Child  — Hooty, Froggy, Green, Olivette
  Baby   — Chea, Hawk, Benny, Perl, Jameel
